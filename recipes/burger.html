How To Make A Burger

  1. Gather Ingredients: buns, patties, lettuce, tomato, cheese, condiments.
  2. Prepare Patties: Season and shape ground beef into patties.
  3. Cook Patties: Grill or pan-fry to desired doneness.
  4. Toast Buns: Lightly toast the burger buns.
  5. Assemble Burger: Layer patties, cheese, lettuce, tomato, and condiments on buns.
  6. Serve: Enjoy your homemade burger!
